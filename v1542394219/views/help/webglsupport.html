Internet Explorer 11 Note
This version of IE11 is missing required WebGL features. Please contact your system administrator to install the latest patches, or use Chrome/Firefox if available.
Firefox Note
WebGL may be supported but disabled in your browser.
Follow these instructions at your own risk!
  1. In a new tab, navigate to 'about:config'.
  2. Type 'webgl' in the search box.
  3. Set 'webgl.disabled' to false.
  4. Set 'webgl.force-enabled' to true.
  5. Refresh {{appName}} and try again.
  6. If you encounter further issues, reset the above to their original values and contact for support.
Google Chrome Note
WebGL may be supported but disabled in your browser.
Follow these instructions at your own risk!
  1. In a new tab, navigate to 'chrome://flags'.
  2. On the right side of the 'Experiments' bar click 'Reset all to default'
  3. On the bottom of the page click the 'Relauch Now' button
  4. If the browser does not re-open on its own, open it manually
  5. When the browser opens, reload {{appName}} and try again.
  6. If you encounter further issues, contact for support.
